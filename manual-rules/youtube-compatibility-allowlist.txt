! ─── AdGuard JS-injection exception ──────────────────────────
!
! Disable all AdGuard inline JS (#%#) on YouTube.
! The inline scripts that cycle through params=eAFgAQ / 8AUB, set
! clientScreen=CHANNEL, and inject adPlaybackContext are the primary
! cause of false Restricted-Mode states.  Because individual inline
! #%# rules cannot be excepted without an exact content match, we
! disable JS-injection site-wide on YouTube.
! Ad blocking is preserved via network-level replace rules, cosmetic
! filters, json-prune scriptlets (uBO), and ad-endpoint blocks.
! (uBlock Origin ignores $jsinject so this only affects AdGuard.)
@@||youtube.com^$jsinject
@@||m.youtube.com^$jsinject
!
